Our Legacy

Our Legacy

Bespoke creations born from the heart, soul, and sweat of our country’s finest innovators in the automotive arts, Jaipur-based custom design workshop Rajputana Custom Motorcycles has an illustrious history in the Indian automotive industry. ‘Rajputana Customs’ workshop is a hyper-creative space with everything from ideation to execution taking place under one roof. In 2016, their design for Harley Davidson and Triumph made it to the top 5 custom motorcycles of the world. their ability to remain grounded will leave you rather perplexed. In fact, as is evident from the very traditional moniker of this company, they prefers to remain true to their roots,

Even going so far as to christen the machines with classic Indian names such as Laado, Aghori, Rajmata, Jordaar, Gulail, Bitoo, nandi, chingari, kaali and so on. This small collection of pictures contains machinists, painters, fabricators, craftsmen, lathe operators and lot of ‘Jugaad Technology’ to look after any complex design requirement such as homemade English wheel and pneumatic planishing hammer.
